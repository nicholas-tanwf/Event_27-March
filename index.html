Built.
Now What?

Anyone can build with AI. But can you launch it? 任何人都能用 AI 做出产品。但你能把它发布出去吗？

Singapore's AI builder community is buzzing — but most products never see a real user. Builder Nights is our way of changing that. We're bringing together people who are building with AI every day for an honest, energetic evening of live demos, real debate, and the conversations that actually help you move from "I built something" to "people are using it." 新加坡的 AI 创造者社区正在蓬勃发展——但大多数产品从未见过真实的用户。Builder Nights 就是我们改变这一现状的方式。我们将每天都在用 AI 构建产品的人聚集在一起，举办充满活力、坦诚真实的夜晚——现场演示、真实辩论，以及那些真正帮你从"我做了个东西"走到"有人在使用它"的对话。

Why this event exists
为什么举办这个活动

ChronoAI builds with AI every single day — and we know first-hand that the hardest part isn't the build. It's everything after. We created Builder Nights to give Singapore's builders a home: a monthly space to share honestly, learn from each other, and be part of a community that is genuinely excited about what AI makes possible. No sponsor agendas. No polished keynotes. Just real people, real products, real conversations. ChronoAI 每天都在用 AI 构建产品——我们深知最难的部分不是构建本身，而是之后的一切。我们创办 Builder Nights，是为了给新加坡的创造者们一个家：一个每月举办的空间，让大家可以坦诚地分享、相互学习，成为一个真正对 AI 可能性感到兴奋的社区的一部分。没有赞助商议程。没有精心打磨的主题演讲。只有真实的人、真实的产品、真实的对话。
