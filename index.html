Built.
Now What?

Anyone can build with AI. But can you launch it?

任何人都能用 AI 做出产品。但你能把它发布出去吗？

Singapore's AI builder community is buzzing — but most products never see a real user. This event brings together people building with AI every day for an honest, energetic evening of live demos, real debate, and conversations that help you move from "I built something" to "people are using it."

新加坡的 AI 创造者社区正在蓬勃发展——但大多数产品从未见过真实的用户。这个活动将每天都在用 AI 构建产品的人聚集在一起，举办充满活力的夜晚——现场演示、真实辩论，以及真正帮你从 "我做了个东西" 走到 "有人在使用它" 的对话。

Why this event exists
为什么举办这个活动

ChronoAI builds with AI every day — and we know first-hand that the hardest part isn't the build. It's everything after. We created this to give Singapore's builders a home: a space to share honestly, learn from each other, and be part of a community genuinely excited about what AI makes possible. No sponsor agendas. No polished keynotes. Real people, real products, real conversations.

ChronoAI 每天都在用 AI 构建——我们深知最难的部分不是构建本身，而是之后的一切。我们创办这个活动，是为了给新加坡的创造者们一个家：坦诚分享、相互学习的空间。没有赞助商议程。没有精心打磨的主题演讲。真实的人、真实的产品、真实的对话。
