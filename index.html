ChronoAI Builder Nights · Edition 1

Built.
Now What?

Anyone can build with AI. But can you launch it?

Singapore's AI builder community is buzzing — but most products never see a real user. Builder Nights is our way of changing that. We're bringing together the people who are building with AI every day for an honest, energetic evening of live demos, real debate, and the conversations that actually help you move from "I built something" to "people are using it."

Why this event exists

ChronoAI builds with AI every single day — and we know first-hand that the hardest part isn't the build. It's everything after. We created Builder Nights to give Singapore's builders a home: a monthly space to share honestly, learn from each other, and be part of a community that is genuinely excited about what AI makes possible. No sponsors pushing agendas. No polished keynotes. Just real people, real products, real conversations.

Who we're here for

Vibe coders building their first app Builders who finished something and don't know what's next Students exploring AI tools for the first time Solopreneurs running on AI automation Practitioners who want honest peer conversations Anyone who believes AI is for builders, not just enterprises
